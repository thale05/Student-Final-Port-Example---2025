Bio

JayTee has been producing on the Montreal scene for three years. She started DJing for friends' parties, but she quickly earned a reputation for her energetic and melodic style and the demand for her music meant that she was soon playing shows around town for a growing audience. She's appeared with artists like Crazed Monarch, Light Without Heat, Distant Son, and Tranquility Race. Her tracks have been featured on radio in Montreal and Toronto, and she'll be appearing at the Montreal Beat Festival along with Dr. Rephlekshun, who features her sounds on his new track, Hate Rebate. Look for her upcoming shows in the Montreal area, come on down, and experience the sound.
